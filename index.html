Welcome to my ePortfolio! I am a Ph.D. student in Educational Technology at the University of Florida, dedicated to integrating AI and big data into the design and research of educational technologies. This website showcases the projects I completed in the course EME6208: Design and Integration of Media Environments 1. This course focuses on learning the principles, methods, and tools for designing and developing multimedia applications—specifically, combining sound, animation, static images, video, and dynamic web technologies in educational technology.
