Image Editing

Part 1 Digital Book Cover




My cover design was inspired by the 2008 movie Waste Allocation Load Lifter: Earth-Class, which tells the story of WALL·E, a robot left behind on Earth to clean up trash. He unexpectedly encounters EVE, a new robot from space, and together they embark on an extraordinary adventure that ultimately impacts the future of humanity. First, I searched for relevant public images. I inserted a background image with standard dimensions, followed by two bot images. I resized and repositioned the two bots, used the Lasso tool to cut them out, and set the alpha channel to 80%. Next, I added text, set its color to red, and adjusted the size to 150. I applied a shadow effect to the text, repositioned the shadow, and changed its color to yellow.




Part 2 Logo Design




To reflect the theme of a chatbot for educational technology, I designed a robot logo. First, I inserted two circles and one rectangle, setting different colors and styles for each. I then used a 3D rectangle to create the robot's arms and the calligraphy tool to draw the eyes and mouth. After that, I added text and stars. Finally, I adjusted the fill and stroke colors for all shapes and text elements.
