Screencast

This screencast was recorded using Camtasia.

In the video, I introduce the advanced search features of Google Scholar, with a focus on the use of Boolean logic.

The video effects primarily include annotation callouts, zoom-in and pan, as well as smooth cursor movements and shadow effects. I also added a goodbye media clip at the end and applied noise reduction to the audio.

Reflection: Screen recording is very useful for me, as many online tutorials require this approach. I feel that annotation and zooming are the two most fundamental techniques, and they seem sufficient for most of my work. In the future, I could try incorporating more elements, along with camera recording and split-screen techniques, to achieve more complex effects.
